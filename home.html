Who We Are

Addendum provides customized residential mortgages for purchasing or refinancing homes. We offer personalized mortgage services such as both FNMA/FHLMC specialty HARP loans, 90% LTV with no mortgage insurance, 95% LTV down to a 580-credit score. We also offer 15-30 year loans, ARM mortgages, fixed mortgages, VA loans, equity lines, FHA loans, conventional loans, jumbo loans, reverse mortgages and USDA loans.

Addendum has been making home buying dreams come true for nearly 20 years. Like many of the people that we help find financial security through their homes, we too come from humble beginnings. In 1996, Addendum began in a small suburb of Arlington in an even smaller office suite. Since then, also like many of those we help find financial security, our standing has improved dramatically.

We are grateful to every one of our borrowers who have allowed us to grow our lending potential over the last two decades. Through providing financial support and services to satisfied homebuyers, Addendum has funded over $4 billion in home loans.
